About Us

Digital Diceworks is a small development team that is currently working towards the standarization of a virtual reality movement system. Our aim is to create a quality gaming experience that not only solves the discomfort of full motion systems but that is complete and fulfilling.


The Staff

Studio Lead: Carlos Molina

"Hello my name is Carlos Molina and I am the Studio Lead for Digital Diceworks. Since we are a small team, I am also the Game Designer/3D Artist/The Man of Many Hats/Not a Programmer. I designed the Natural Locomotion system as an answer for the need to create a VR motion system that doesn't cause players discomfort but gives the freedom to explore without breaking immersion"




The Progammer: Ronnie Smith

Ronnie Smith is a Generalist Game Programmer with many years of professional expereience. He has been involved in various indie projects through the years. He has worked for Heartwood Inc creating training experiences using Unity 3D as their technical foundation for many years. Ronnie has dabbled with many platforms and hardware including iOS, android, webGL, tango, cardboard, daydream and vive.






Level Designer: Johannes Robbins

Johannes is a Level Designer with over 10 years of professional experience and over 16 years of modification experience. He has been involved in projects ranging from portable console titles with 3 man teams to Xbox/Playstation games to high end PC MMO games that take 250 people and multiple years to make. He has worked for AAA companies like SEGA, EA, Gazillion as well as small startups. He is also a VR evangelist and has been obsessed about VR hardware since the late 1990s.
